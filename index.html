FAQ

Common questions

Do you work with all booking platforms?

Yes! Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, direct bookings, and more. We can sync with any compatible calendar.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We ask for at least 12 hours' notice when possible. We understand the nature of short-term rentals and try to be as flexible as possible.

Do I need to be present during the clean?

Not at all. Most of our hosts give us a lockbox or keypad code. We handle everything and send you a photo report when we're done.

What supplies do you bring?

We bring all professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. If you have preferred products or specific requirements, just let us know.

Can I book recurring cleans on a set schedule?

Yes, and we recommend it. Regular clients get priority scheduling and preferred rates.

Are you insured?

Yes. We carry full general liability insurance and all staff are background-checked.
