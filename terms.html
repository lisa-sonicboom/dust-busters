About us

KleenBNB provides professional Airbnb turnover and deep cleaning services in Porto, Portugal. These terms apply to all services booked with us. By making a booking you agree to these terms.

Our services

Our standard turnover clean includes a full clean of the property, linen change, and consumables restock. Deep cleans and additional services are available at the rates set out in our current rate card. The exact scope of each service is confirmed at the time of booking.

Bookings

Bookings can be made by WhatsApp, email, or through our website contact form. A booking is confirmed once we send written confirmation. We reserve the right to decline a booking at our discretion.

Pricing and payment

All prices are quoted in euros and include applicable taxes. Prices are based on property size as set out in our rate card. Add-on services are charged in addition to the base rate. Invoices are issued per clean or monthly, as agreed. Payment is due within 7 days of the invoice date. Late payments may incur an interest charge in accordance with Portuguese law.

Cancellations and changes

We require a minimum of 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a booking without charge. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged at 50% of the agreed rate. Express bookings (made with less than 4 hours' notice) are subject to the express surcharge set out in our rate card.
